MECHATRONICS DEPARTMENT

PROFILE

Sanjivani College of Engineering has a legacy to provide best in engineering education since 1983. A strong industry connect , alumina , futuristic cognitive skill demand of industry 4.0, robotics, IoT, AI, automotive automation and home appliance automation suggest a scope of ‘Mechatronics’ in engineering education. Sanjivani college of engineering understand this industrial and societal demand of automation and forward a step ahead with a new beginning of ‘Mechatronics’ branch in engineering from 2020-21. ‘Mechatronics’ is a synergic integration of Computer science, Mechanical , Electrical, & control engineering.

Mission of Department

Our vision is to develop mechatronics engineering professionals to cater real time challenges of globe through competency based Mechatronics Engineering Education.

To offer high-quality competency based Mechatronics Engineering Education and prepare learners to become innovators, entrepreneurs and technocrats. To contribute to research and discovery in real time interdisciplinary challenges of mechatronics engineering arenas. To train learners in the learning skills, literacy skills, life skills, professional skills, citizen values, and ethics to upgrade the quality of life & professional careers. To promote the establishment of ‘knowledge hub’ in emerging areas of technology, nurturing innovative ideas and kindle creativity among faculty and students.
