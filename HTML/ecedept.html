ELECTRONICS AND COMPUTER DEPARTMENT

PROFILE

The Universe that we live in is continuously shrinking and real credit goes to the fast growing Electronics and Telecommunications revolution. The Electronics Engineering Department was established in 1983 and was later converted to Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.The department has been declared as a NODAL CENTRE of the e-Prayog, Virtual labs (Electronics) IIT Bombay. Under this scheme various workshops on virtual laboratories have been organized. The department has also been granted the status of a SUPER RESOURCE CENTRE for Spoken Tutorial Project, IIT Bombay under the ‘National Mission on Education through ICT’, MHRD, Govt. of India.

Vision of Department

Mission of Department

To produce quality professionals in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering with knowledge and skill sets to meet diversifying needs of industry and society.

To impart the technology of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering through effective teaching-learning process. To establish linkages between industry and academia for overall development of students. To promote innovative ideas in solving multi-disciplinary engineering problems having social relevance. To develop technical human resources exhibiting professional and ethical attitudes
